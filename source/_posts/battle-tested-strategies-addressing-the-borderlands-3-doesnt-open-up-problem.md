### Fix 1\. Check your system requirement

| OS:         | Windows 2000, XP, Vista, 8, or 10                                            |
| ----------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Processor:  | Intel Pentium 4 1.5 GHz or Athlon XP 1500+                                   |
